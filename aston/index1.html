Company.

Aston Martin Lagonda Limited is a British manufacturer of luxury sports cars and grand tourers. It was founded in 1913 by Lionel Martin and Robert Bamford. The company has had a chequered financial history, including bankruptcy in the 1970s, but has also enjoyed long periods of success and stability, including under the ownership of David Brown, from 1947 to 1972 and of the Ford Motor Company from 1994 to 2007.

Aston Martin of North America More like out surf spot.

Irvine, CA, 9 January 2015 - Aston Martin is set to add to the company’s dealership reach across North America after finalising new dealer appointments in Minneapolis and Nashville, increasing the company’s total footprint to 46 retail sites across the Americas region; part of a 21% growth in dealership presence in the United States over the last two years.

Aston Martin Minneapolis will be led by new dealer partner, Morries Automotive Group with a temporary facility in Hopkins, Minnesota. Work is underway to launch a new permanent Aston Martin showroom space in Golden Valley, Minnesota this summer.

Aston Martin Nashville sees a new partnership with Carlock Management Company to launch an initial Aston Martin facility in Franklin, Tennessee. A new permanent Aston Martin showroom in Brentwood, located in Williamson County, Tennessee will follow before the end of 2015.

Both new appointments are open for sales business immediately with full servicing capabilities to cater for current and future Aston Martin owners scheduled to be available in the coming weeks. The establishment of these new dealer partners mean 85% of the HLS (High Luxury Sports) market in the United States now resides within 100 miles of an Aston Martin dealership.

Features.

ENGINE.AN ALL NEW ENGINE AM11

The New DB9 is powered by our all-new AM11 engine, the most powerful in DB9's history. Its 6.0-litre capacity delivers 517 PS, a 0-62 mph time of just 4.6 secs and 620 Nm of torque - a 10% increase on our previous model..

DESIGN.DRIVEN BY DESIGN

The New DB9 was not driven by legislation, but by design. A key principle: simplicity. We didn’t use heavy, bulky architecture and systems, we created discreet solutions, elegant solutions, Aston Martin solutions..

STANDARD EQUIPMENT.NOT NOISE. JUST SOUND

Like the near-perfect structural balance of DB9 we’ve also achieved a perfect balance of interior and exterior sound. The New AM11 engine is tuned specifically by Aston Martin sound engineers to make for the best driving experience.
BREAKTHROUGH SUSPENSION TECHNOLOGY

To create a car which felt and performed better than the previous DB9 we engineered a new suspension system, Gen4, for the New DB9. Aston Martin engineers delivered breakthrough technology with a state-of-the-art three stage Adaptive Damping System (ADS). 'Normal' 'Sport' and 'Track' modes allow the driver to tailor the New DB9 to their personal preference with instant adjustments to conditions, road surface and speed.
